Pig Dice

A simple dice game with two players.


Rules

  • Enter player name.
  • Start
  • Player 1 rolls, has 3 turns. Hold.
  • If rolls a 1, lost. Turn for player 2.
  • If satisfied with draw hold to cumulate points.
  • First 100 points wins.

Goodluck!!
